# Fallow Suppression Rationale Registry

Reserved location for rationale entries corresponding to config-level fallow suppressions (`ignoreExports`, `ignoreDependencies`, `ignorePatterns`, `usedClassMembers`).

This file's presence is the capability surface. Whether entries are required here, and in what format, is a policy decision set by a separate change (tracked as `apply-ts-suppression-rationale`).
